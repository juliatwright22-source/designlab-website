Our Process

From your first conversation to a live website — here's exactly how we work.

1

Free

Free Consultation Call

We start with a free 30-minute call. You tell us about your business, your preferred colours and typography, who your customers are, and what you need from your website. No preparation needed — just show up and chat.

2

Free

Free Mockup

Based on your consultation, we design a custom mockup and send it to you at no cost. This gives you a real visual before you commit to anything. No templates, no guesswork — designed specifically for your business.

3

$100

Revision Call

If you'd like to move forward, you pay a flat $100 fee for a follow-up call. We go over the mockup together, answer all your questions, discuss any changes, and finalise the direction before a single line of code is written.

4

$500–$5,000

Website Build

Once the plan is locked in, we build. Static sites take 1–2 weeks and start at $500. Dynamic sites with booking systems, CMS, or custom features start at $2,000 and typically take 3–6 weeks.

Free

Review & Invoice

We show you the finished website. You review it, request any final tweaks, and give us the green light. Only once you're happy do we send the invoice. You love it first — you pay after.

Optional — $30/month

Monthly Maintenance

After launch, we offer an optional ongoing maintenance plan: bug fixes, small content updates, security checks, and peace of mind that someone is looking after your site so you don't have to.

Packages & Pricing

Both tiers include a free consultation and free mockup before you spend a dollar.

Static Website

Best for: Local businesses, freelancers, personal brands Timeline: Approximately 1–2 weeks

A static website is fast, reliable, and professional. It's the perfect starting point for any small business that needs a strong online presence — whether that's a landing page, a portfolio, or a multi-page business site.
